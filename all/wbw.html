What's Your Status

What’s Your Status, a web series of 3 different people, going through the 3 different phases of relationships- single, committed and married. Being single is awesome, and so is being in a relationship but life becomes quite awkward for those who are the only one single in their circle. Meet Baljinder aka Balu (Manjot Singh), a single MBA student in the quest to find the girl of his dreams. Will he be able to find his one true love and finally change his relationship status?
